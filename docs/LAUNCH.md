# Taproot POS — V1.0 Launch Kit 🌿

## Product Hunt

**Title:** Taproot POS — the POS that reads your menu and sets itself up

**Tagline:** Upload your menu PDF. Live in 10 minutes. No contract, no hardware, no surprises.

**Description:**
> Taproot is a modern point-of-sale built for the 500,000 independent restaurants that Toast and
> Square overlook. Upload a menu PDF or photo and our AI builds your entire catalog in ~60 seconds —
> prices, categories, modifiers, the works. Then run the whole business from any device you already
> own: fast checkout, offline mode, kitchen display, table service & QR ordering, loyalty, gift
> cards, discounts, multi-location, and an AI intelligence layer (demand forecasting, staff
> scheduling, menu engineering, food-cost, a daily briefing, and a copilot you can ask anything).
>
> $199/mo flat per location. Everything included. Cancel anytime. Your data is always yours.

**First comment (maker):** Why we built it — independent operators deserve enterprise-grade tools
without enterprise contracts or proprietary hardware. AI setup turns an 8-hour POS migration into a
coffee break.
